About Derrick’s Urban Kicks

Founded by Derrick in December 2025, Derrick’s Urban Kicks is Nairobi’s premier destination for luxury urban footwear. From our headquarters in Kahawa West, we craft and curate sneakers that blend contemporary street style with premium quality, offering every customer a statement of confidence and distinction.

More than shoes, Derrick’s Urban Kicks represents a lifestyle where elegance meets urban culture, and every step reflects individuality. With country-wide delivery, our exclusive collections reach customers across Kenya, ensuring style and sophistication are never out of reach.
